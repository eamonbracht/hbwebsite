Aluminum Flat Top Dolly

H&B's 2.5 Ton Capacity Aluminum Flat Top Dolly is our most popular general purpose dolly.
Constructed from structural 6061 aluminum, it engineered to be light without compromising strength or quality. Capable of rolling smoothly and easily even at full capacity, it is ideal for everything from machinery moving to movie sets.

2 Ton Skate Dollies

H&B's 2 Ton Capacity Steel Dollies is our most popular and versatile portable lifting solution.
Constructed from structural 6061 aluminum, this mid-sized gantry comes in 3 different models to suit your unique height requriements. It can be set up by one person and lift loads of up to 4,000 lbs.
