1 Ton Capacity Portable Aluminum Gantry

H&B's 1 Ton Capacity Portable Aluminum Gantry is our lightest and most portable mobile lifting solution.
Constructed from strong but light 6061 aluminum, this small but mighty gantry can be set up by one person and lift loads of up to 2,000 lbs.

2 Ton Capacity Portable Aluminum Gantry

H&B's 2 Ton Capacity Portable Aluminum Gantry is our most popular and versatile portable lifting solution.
Constructed from strong but light 6061 aluminum, this mid-sized gantry comes in 3 different models to suit your unique height requriements. It can be set up by one person and lift loads of up to 4,000 lbs.

2 Ton Capacity 4 Angle Portable Aluminum Gantry

H&B's 2 Ton Capacity 4 Angle Portable Aluminum Gantry is a variation on our popular 2 Ton gantry, but for those looking to span up to 14' vertically.
Constructed from strong but light 6061 aluminum, this study gantry is perfect for those looking for strength and safety at extreme heights. It can be set up by one person and lift loads of up to 4,000 lbs to an incredible 14'.

3 Ton Capacity Portable Aluminum Gantry

H&B's 3 Ton Capacity Portable Aluminum Gantry is a set up from our 2 Ton models, for those who need to lift more in the same sized package.
Constructed from strong but light 6061 aluminum, it delivers higher strengnth in the same form factor. It can be set up by one person and lift loads of up to 6,000 lbs.
