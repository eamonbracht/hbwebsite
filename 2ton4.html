2 Ton Capacity Portable Aluminum Gantry 4 Angle Bracing

H&B's 2 Ton Capacity 4 Angle Portable Aluminum Gantry is a variation on our popular 2 Ton gantry, but for those looking to span up to 14' vertically.
Constructed from strong but light 6061 aluminum, this study gantry is perfect for those looking for strength and safety at extreme heights. It can be set up by one person and lift loads of up to 4,000 lbs to an incredible 14'.
