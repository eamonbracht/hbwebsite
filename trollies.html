JET® Manual Gantry Trolly (1, 2, 3, 5 Ton Capacity)

H&B is proud to be an official distributor of Jet® Manual Trollies. We certify our gantries capacity using these trollies and are proud to offer them standard with all our gantries or sell them individually.

Call 312-829-4850 to request a quote.

Technical Details

JET® HDT Series manual trolleys are available in capacities from 1 ton to 5 Tons. They are designed for low maintenance, trouble free use on wide flange H-beams, standard I-beams or curved track. Each JET® Manual Trolley conforms to OSHA regulations, and ANSI / ASME standards.
