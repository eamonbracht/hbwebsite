2 Ton Portable Aluminum Gantry

Flat top dollies are ideal for handling skidded or crated machinery, large-form pieces of art and other heavy materials. Equipped with heavy-duty swivel casters for easy rolling and long wear with all welded construction.

All our dollies have plwyood tops which extends the life span of this product considerably. This general use dolly is beloved by our customers who constantly find useful applications for this quality dolly.
